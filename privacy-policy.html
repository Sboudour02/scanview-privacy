Privacy Policy for ScanView Pro

Last Updated: May 2026

TL;DR: ScanView Pro is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit your personal data. All processing happens entirely locally on your device. We do not use ads, analytics, or third-party SDKs.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ScanView Pro ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. Our core philosophy is strict privacy by design. We believe your data belongs to you, which is why ScanView Pro operates completely offline and never transmits your data to any external servers.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal data. ScanView Pro operates locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we track your usage, scan history, or generated barcodes.

3. Device Permissions

To function properly, ScanView Pro requires certain device permissions. These permissions are used exclusively for core application features and never for data collection or tracking:

  • Camera: Required solely to scan barcodes and QR codes. The video feed is processed entirely locally on your device in real-time. No images or video frames are ever saved, recorded, or transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Storage / Local File Handling: Required to save generated barcodes to your device's storage or to select existing images from your gallery for scanning. The application only accesses the specific files you explicitly choose to interact with.

4. Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party SDKs

ScanView Pro is built to be a premium, secure utility. To guarantee your privacy, we ensure that:

  • We do not include any advertising networks or frameworks (No Ads).
  • We do not use any telemetry, crash reporting, or user tracking analytics.
  • We do not integrate any third-party data collection SDKs.

5. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of your scanned and generated data relies on your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date and utilizing standard device security measures (like a passcode or biometric lock).

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, our application is completely safe for all ages, including children under the age of 13. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global privacy regulations.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app does not collect data or connect to our servers, we will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and through the app's standard update release notes in the app store.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support email provided in the official app store listing.
